Hey Travern

A few years back while walking home from a late night shift out in industrial Brooklyn, I was greeted by a man; by name. "Hey Man" I said briefly and rudely brushed him off to catch the last train headed uptown to the Bronx. So what? I know that´s what you’re thinking. Problem is I don’t remember him… all (I have great memory; especially w/ faces). The bigger issue is that, he called me by my government name which no one; not even my parents use to address me. Perhaps we met at a sporting event, school or maybe our paths crossed when I was attempting to pull off one of my shenanigans. Yeah that´s it as I mumbled to myself that seemed to be the only logical option I could think of.
